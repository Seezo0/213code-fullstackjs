Fluffy Pancakes Recipe

These soft and fluffy pancakes are perfect for a quick and delicious breakfast. They are easy to make and require simple ingredients you likely already have at home.

Preparation Time

  • Prep Time: 5 Minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 Minutes
  • Total Time: 15 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup all-purpose floud
  • Tablespoon sugar
  • 1 Cup milk

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour and sugar, then add milk and stir until smooth.
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat and pour small portions of batter.
  3. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook the other side until golden.
